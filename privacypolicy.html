Progress Evolved Privacy Policy

Progress Evolved stores body measurements, progress photos, notes, journals, goals, reminders, Health Connect data you choose to import, and purchase/remove-ads state.

The app is local-first. Progress Evolved does not upload tracker history, photos, notes, Health Connect imports, or backup contents to Progress Evolved servers - we do not even have any servers. All app data is stored only on your phone and never leaves the device except when you explicitly choose to export, share, or save a backup to a location you control.

Photos

The app can access photos when you take a progress photo, import selected images, or enable album watch. Album watch uses Android photo-library permission to find new images in chosen albums. You can disable album import in Settings.

Health Data

With your permission, Progress Evolved can read weight, body fat, steps, height, and historical Health Connect data. Optional write-back can write weight, body fat, and steps. You can revoke Health Connect permissions in Android settings.

Backups And Exports

You can export CSV files, tagged photo bundles, reports, and password-encrypted .pebackup backups. These files are created only when you choose to create them, and they leave the phone only if you save or share them to a chosen location such as cloud storage, another app, or external storage. Backup passwords are chosen by you and are required to restore backup contents. The app cannot recover a forgotten backup password.

Ads And Purchases

The Android app may show ads and offers a remove-ads purchase. Ad providers and app stores may process identifiers, purchase status, diagnostics, or fraud-prevention signals according to their own policies.

Deletion

You can delete photos, entries, Health-synced values, or all app data from Settings. Deleted entries and photos may remain in the app trash temporarily before permanent deletion.
